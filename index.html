About

A traditional home
on the Samurai Road

Your ideal base for hiking the historic Nakasendo between Magome-juku and Tsumago-juku — two of Japan's best-preserved post towns, easily accessible on foot.


Tatami rooms, futon bedding, a private wooden deck. The entire house is yours. Not a hotel — an authentic Japanese home to return to after a day on the trail.
